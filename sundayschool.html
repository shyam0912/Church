Sunday School


“ Start children off on the way, they should go, and even when they are old they will not turn from it”.
Proverbs 22:6

Sunday school is a prerequisite in every church to bring up our young ones in the light of the word of God. We wish to sow seeds of faith into the hearts of our children. Our church yearns for children who crave God’s love.
To watch our children grow into fruitful vines is all we aspire. We truly believe that no child should be left behind, no matter where they are from. It’s our goal to help each child affirm his or her faith in our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.
And as a part of the CSI congregation, we take pride in our Sunday school movements.
➢ Sunday school classes are now held online due to the current situation. Links are given through respective WhatsApp class groups.
✓ Time: 4 pm – 4:45 pm
